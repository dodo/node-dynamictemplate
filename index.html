dynamic templating engine

Yet another template engine, but this time loaded with full async support and capable of being changed even after the template was rendered.

With less WTF's:

  • no searching in DOM
  • no own template-language
  • no scope breaking
  • no evil eval
  • no need for tons of #ids

With Hipster Features:

  • capable of writing and maintaining real-time user interfaces
  • handles large DOM manipulations for you as animation
  • abstraction and separation of functionality and design
  • runs on server and browser side
  • streamable output
  • completely asynchronous
  • modular
  • smart
  • sunglasses

Writing user interfaces where your data comes in in real-time can haz multiple obsticles. don't let them hit you. use Δt.
